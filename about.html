About Game Dev Palmy

Palmy Game Dev is a vibrant community of game developers located in Palmerston North. Our mission is to support and foster the growth of game development in our region by providing resources, organizing events, and creating a network of passionate developers.

Our Mission

We aim to create an inclusive environment where developers of all skill levels can collaborate, learn, and grow together. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, Game Dev Palmy offers opportunities for networking, skill development, and project collaboration.
